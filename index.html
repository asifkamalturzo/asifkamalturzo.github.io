Asif Kamal Turzo, PhD

Assistant Professor,
Department of Computer & Information Science,
University of Massachusetts Dartmouth

Dr. Asif Kamal Turzo is an Assistant Professor in the Computer and Information Science department at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. He has completed his Ph.D. from Wayne State University under the supervision of Dr. Amiangshu Bosu. He has completed his Master's from Wayne State University in 2022. He worked as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Computer Science department at Idaho State University. His research interest lies in empirical software engineering, software security, human aspects of software engineering, machine learning, natural language processing, and artificial intelligence. He has published research works in top-tier conferences and journals in the software engineering field, including ICSE, FSE, ASE, ESEM, APSEC, TSE, EMSE, and TOSEM.
